Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Spendly collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our personal expense tracking application. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the service. This includes:

  • Account information: Your name and email address, provided when you register
  • Authentication data: Your password, stored in hashed form — we never store plain-text passwords
  • Expense data: The expense records you create, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions
  • Usage data: Basic information about how you interact with the application, such as pages visited and actions taken, used only for improving the service

We do not collect payment information, government identification, or any sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

The information we collect is used solely to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Display your expense records and generate summaries
  • Authenticate your identity when you sign in
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve application performance
  • Respond to support requests or inquiries you submit

We do not use your data to serve advertisements, build behavioral profiles, or for any purpose beyond operating the service.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a local SQLite database. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. These measures include:

  • Password hashing using a secure one-way algorithm
  • Access controls that restrict data to the authenticated account owner
  • No transmission of personal data to external third-party servers

While we strive to protect your information, no method of data storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, and you use the service at your own risk.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

Spendly uses session cookies to keep you logged in during your visit. These cookies are temporary and are cleared when you close your browser or log out. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or any third-party cookies. No persistent identifiers are stored on your device beyond what is required for session management.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties. Your expense data is private to your account and is not accessible to other users or external organizations. The only exceptions would be:

  • If required by law, court order, or a valid legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Spendly or its users

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and expense data for as long as your account remains active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal information and expense records from our systems within a reasonable time, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Access: You can view all expense data stored in your account at any time by logging in
  • Correction: You can edit or update your expense records and account details within the application
  • Deletion: You can delete individual expense records or request full account deletion by contacting us
  • Portability: You may request an export of your data in a structured format

8. Children's Privacy

Spendly is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us through the support channel available within the application. We will respond to all reasonable requests in a timely manner.
